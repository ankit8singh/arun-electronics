Return & Refund Policy

Easy returns and quick refunds for your satisfaction

Return Policy

7-Day Return Window

You can return most items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund.

Condition Requirements

Items must be in original condition - unused, undamaged, and in original packaging.

Defective Products

Defective or damaged products can be returned within 15 days of delivery.

Non-Returnable Items

Electrical wires, cut materials, and installed products cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons.

Refund Policy

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item.

Payment Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during purchase.

Shipping Charges

Original shipping charges are non-refundable. Return shipping is customer's responsibility.

How to Return an Item

1
Contact Support

Message us on WhatsApp with your order details and reason for return

2
Get Return Approval

We'll verify your request and provide return instructions

3
Pack the Item

Pack the item securely in original packaging with all accessories

4
Ship or Drop Off

You can ship the item or drop it at our store location

5
Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund

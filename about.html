Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Arun, Arun Electric Solutions started as a small electrical repair shop with a vision to provide reliable and affordable electrical services to the local community. What began as a one-person operation has now grown into a trusted electrical service provider with a team of skilled electricians.

Arun's passion for electrical work and commitment to quality has been the driving force behind our success. With over 5 years of experience in the electrical industry, we have completed hundreds of projects ranging from simple repairs to complete house wiring and decorative lighting installations.

"Good wiring today saves big problems tomorrow. We believe in doing the job right the first time, ensuring safety and reliability for years to come."

- Arun, Founder
